How Does It Work?


Answers to Common Questions

What does canopy care do?
We provide pediatric health services through telepresence and in-home visits. This includes consultations, diagnoses, management, treatment, and prescriptions.

How much does canopy care cost?
Membership to Canopy Care costs $60 per month (plus tax). All tele-health services are provided with the membership. In-home visits cost $120/month (plus tax).

Do we accept insurance?
No, we do not accept insurance. However, depending on your insurance plan, canopy care payments may count toward your deductible or be reimbursed by your FSA/HSA.

How do I join?
Fill out the form above. We will get in touch with you to set up your membership.

How do I reach my provider?
Contact your provider directly on their personal phone and e-mail. When you need to see them face-to-face, they will come to you.

When can I reach my provider?
Anytime! No appointments or screenings required.
